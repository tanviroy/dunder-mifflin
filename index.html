    About Us

    A legacy in the paper-selling industry. With titans like Michael Scott, along with brilliant emerging talents like Ryan Howard, our Dundie-winning team offers the best paper buying experience any customer could only dream of.

    Lasting Impact

    We serve every customer with only the very best. Quality product plus quality service that's the Dunder Mifflin way. Our mission is to ensure that one day, none of the poor, starving kids in Africa will have to worry about their daily paper needs.

    Diverse Workspace

    We believe in creating equal opportunities for everyone. Our team sports some extremely talented men *and* women that spend every living minute trying to spread the joy of paper across the globe. Seriously. We love diversity! We love Mexicans and homosexuals!

    Serious Commitment

    We are a 'No nonsense' group that believes nothing says seriousness better than some good ol' fun. Some day, when a kid calls his mother in the middle of the night because he's scared, she'll say "Don't worry. If any monsters try to eat you up, Dunder Mifflin will save you". This is our vision.

    Services

    We sell paper. And paper is the lifeblood of society. So basically, we sell lives.

    Paper is Forever

    Sure, computers are great for silly games and forwarding funny emails about your boss. But real business is done on paper.

    Experienced Staff

    Our employees have personally coordinated and implemented receipt, storage, and delivery of over 2.5 billion units of inventory (read: pieces of paper).

    Delivered to you

    The best paper money could buy. Why go to Staples and buy ordinary white paper when you can purchase directly from us and get great paper delivered to your home.

    The Golden Ticket

    If you ever receive a delivery from us, look out for a Golden Ticket! *Each* ticket will make you eligible for a exclusive 10% discount on your next purchase.

    Important Accounts

    We are the official paper suppliers of the Lackawanna County, as well as large companies such as the Blue Cross of Pennsylvania.

    Career Opportunities

    We are constantly on the lookout for young (and old) hardworkers willing to join our Dundie-winning team. We offer internships and entry level jobs to all people regardless of gender, ethnicity and sexual orientation.
